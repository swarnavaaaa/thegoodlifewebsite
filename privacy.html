Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. Information We Collect

At Good Life Counseling Services, we prioritize your privacy and confidentiality. We only collect information that is necessary to provide you with the best possible care. This may include personal identification information (name, email address, phone number) when you voluntarily submit it through our contact forms or when scheduling an appointment.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information collected is used strictly for the purpose of communicating with you, scheduling appointments, and managing your care. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties without your explicit consent, except where required by law.

3. Confidentiality and HIPAA Compliance

All clinical communications and records are maintained in strict accordance with HIPAA regulations and professional ethical standards. Details regarding the limits of confidentiality will be discussed thoroughly during your initial consultation.

4. Cookies and Website Tracking

Our website may use "cookies" to enhance your user experience. Your web browser places cookies on your hard drive for record-keeping purposes and sometimes to track information. You may choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent.
