About Me

I’m Yash, a counselling psychologist.

Most people who come to me are not unaware—they’ve already thought a lot about what’s going on.

The problem is that despite all that thinking, they still feel stuck.

You might be:

→ Overthinking things but not reaching clarity

→ Feeling anxious, low, or mentally drained

→ Struggling with stress from work, studies, or expectations

→ Dealing with relationship or family situations that feel confusing or difficult

→ Knowing something isn’t right, but not knowing what to do about it

At some point, it stops being about the problem itself—and becomes about feeling stuck with it. That’s where I come in.

I help you step out of that confusion, understand what’s actually happening, and respond in a way that is clearer and more grounded.

This is not just a space to talk—it’s a space to make sense of things. We break things down, identify patterns, and work towards clarity you can actually use.
