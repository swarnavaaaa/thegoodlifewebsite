How I Work

Therapy here is not just about talking through what's happening.

I focus on helping you step out of emotional overwhelm and understand your thoughts, reactions, and situations more clearly—so you're not just caught in them, but able to respond more deliberately.

At times, this means making sense of difficult emotions like confusion, heaviness, or feeling low. At other times, it involves breaking down patterns in your thinking that keep you stuck.

The goal is not just insight—but clarity you can actually use in your day-to-day life.

The aim is not to stay in therapy—but to reach a point where you can think and respond more clearly on your own.

We work together to:

  • ✔ Slow things down so they feel less overwhelming
  • ✔ Understand what you're feeling and why
  • ✔ Identify patterns in how you think and respond
  • ✔ Step out of repetitive mental loops
  • ✔ Build clearer, more grounded ways of responding
